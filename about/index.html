ABOUT ME

 
 
 
 
 

Clinical Postdoctoral Fellow

JFK Partners, University of Colorado, Anschutz Medical Campus

Sep 2023 – Present Aurora, CO
I am completing my postdoctoral training in psychology at JFK Partners, as a LEND trainee. I conduct asssessments on a multidisciplinary team, attend LEND trainee activities, provide therapy to children with autism / IDD, and am involved in the FYF-IDD research project. My mentors and clinical supervisors are Dr. Judy Reaven and Dr. Audrey Blakeley-Smith.
 
 
 
 
 

Psychology Doctoral Intern

Clifford Beers

Sep 2022 – Aug 2023 New Haven, CT
I completed my internship at Clifford Beers Community Care Center (APA accredited), which is a community mental health center. I provided outpatient therapy, and comprehensive diagnostic assessments for autism, ADHD, IDD, and other mental health concerns (e.g., PTSD, anxiety, depression).
 
 
 
 
 

PhD Student

University of Oregon

Sep 2018 – Sep 2023 University of Oregon
I completed my PhD in School Psychology at the University of Oregon (APA-accredited). My graduate advisors were Dr. Laura Lee McIntyre and Dr. Nicole Guiliani. My dissertation focused on how parent-child emotion regulation processes explained the relationship between parenting stress and mental health correlates in preschoolers with DD. My clinical experiences included conducting evaluations across school and clinic settings, and providing evidence-based therapy in a range of settings (clinics, community programs, telehealth, schools) and populations (foster parents, individuals with DD/autism/ADHD, LGBTQ+ teens).
 
 
 
 
 

Research Assistant

Duke Center for Autism and Brain Development

Jun 2016 – Aug 2018 Durham, NC
Post college, I worked on research projects led by Drs. Kimberly Carpenter and Geraldine Dawson. My main involvement was with a project investigating the neurobiological mechanisms underlying the relationship between sensory over-responsivity and anxiety in autistic preschoolers. I also assisted with the processing of MRI, EEG, and EGT data, as well as an ongoing Autism Center of Excellence grant investigating the relationship between autism and ADHD.
